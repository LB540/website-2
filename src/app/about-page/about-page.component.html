About

Edge Guard is a podcast about video games on the fringe: games at the unexplored edges of itch.io, student games, experimental releases, and more. Each week, two friends—one with a degree in game design, one studying games in an English grad program—play and discuss a different game from the edges of gaming, focusing on what their developers get right rather than what doesn’t quite work. From intresting fun to the productively weird, Edge Guard finds games worth playing.

Meet Blake

Hi, my name's Blake! I graduated from the Entertainment Arts Engineering program at the University of Utah with a degree in Computer Science with an Emphasis in Game Design. I've designed a handful of games of my own, and I love to apply the knowledge I have of game design to the analysis of every game I play. I believe that criticism is one of the most powerful tools we have for bettering our society, and I seek to apply that ethos to every episode of this show. I also make music occasionally (like our theme song!) and I even built this very website! Follow me on Twitter!
