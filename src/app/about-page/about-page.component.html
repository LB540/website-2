About the Podcast:
Edge Guard is a podcast about video games on the fringe: games at the unexplored edges of itch.io, student games, experimental releases, and more. Each week, two friends—one with a degree in game design, one studying games in an English grad program—play and discuss a different game from the edges of gaming, focusing on what their developers get right rather than what doesn’t quite work. From intresting fun to the productively weird, Edge Guard finds games worth playing.
Where to find us:
Find us on iTunes, Spotify, Google Play, Stitcher, or hit our RSS feed directly from your favorite podcast app. Also, make sure to follow us on Twitter @EdgeGuardCast for our latest updates and links to the games we play.
